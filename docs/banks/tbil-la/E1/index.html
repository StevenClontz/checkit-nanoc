Example 1

Consider the augmented matrix

\[ \left[\begin{array}{cccc|c} -2 & 1 & -8 & -5 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 & 5 & 3 & 0 \\ 0 & -3 & 6 & 3 & 3 \end{array}\right] \]

  1. Write a system of scalar equations corresponding to this augmented matrix.
  2. Write a vector equation corresponding to this augmented matrix.

Answer:

  1. \begin{alignat*}{5} -2 \, x_{1} &+& x_{2} &-& 8 \, x_{3} &-& 5 \, x_{4} &=& 1 \\x_{1} &-& x_{2} &+& 5 \, x_{3} &+& 3 \, x_{4} &=& 0 \\ &-& 3 \, x_{2} &+& 6 \, x_{3} &+& 3 \, x_{4} &=& 3 \\ \end{alignat*}

  2. \[ x_{1} \left[\begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array}\right] + x_{2} \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ -1 \\ -3 \end{array}\right] + x_{3} \left[\begin{array}{c} -8 \\ 5 \\ 6 \end{array}\right] + x_{4} \left[\begin{array}{c} -5 \\ 3 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] = \left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 3 \end{array}\right] \]
