G1 - Row operations and determinants

Example 1

Let \(A\) be a \(4 \times 4\) matrix with determinant \( 3 \).

  1. Let \(C\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_4 \to R_4 + 2R_1 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ C\)?
  2. Let \(Q\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \leftrightarrow R_2 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ Q\)?
  3. Let \(B\) be the matrix obtained from \(A\) by applying the row operation \( R_3 \to -2R_3 \). What is \(\operatorname{det}\ B\)?

Answer:

  1. \(\operatorname{det}\ C= 3 \)
  2. \(\operatorname{det}\ Q= -3 \)
  3. \(\operatorname{det}\ B= -6 \)
