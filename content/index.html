☑️It

Free and open randomized exercises for practice and assessment.

For students: Use these exercises to ✔️ your understanding of your course's objectives.

For instructors: Use these exercises in assessments to help you ✔️ on student understanding.

Looking to author your own randomized exercises?

The ☑️It platform runs completely inside your web browser, powered by CoCalc.com! Try out the Tutorial file to get started. (CoCalc trial accounts take some time to load and compute, so consider signing up and purchasing a subscription if you like the app.)

Our community of authors and developers is organized in the #checkit-app channel of the Mastery Grading Slack workspace. Come join us!

Add this site as an app on your phone

On Android/Chrome: tap the browser menu, then tap "Add to Home screen".

On iOS/Safari: tap the share arrow, then tap "Add to Home Screen".
